Types of Window Blinds

Blinds are some of the most versatile window treatments available. They can make a room feel warm and inviting. They can add a modern twist or a classical element. And, with both tilt and lift options, they can help control how much light you want in your room. Customize your window blinds to fit your needs by finding the right upgrades for your home or office. Possible upgrades include:

  • • Routeless slats give you better light control and easier maintenance and cleaning.
  • • Cloth tapes are a fashionable way to prevent light leakage and add a flair of style.
  • • The continuous cord loop makes heavy blinds on wider windows easier to lift.
  • • Cordless controls or motorized tilt offer a more seamless look and provide hassle-free operation.

Wood Binds

Nothing is more classic or more elegant than natural wood blinds. Our selection features real North American hardwood handcrafted to your custom specifications. Choose between 1”, 2” and 2 1/2” slats as well as a wide array of rich stains and fresh paint colors. Wood blinds are a great choice for living rooms, bedrooms and offices. To avoid warping and cracking, wood blinds should not be placed in high humidity rooms like bathrooms or garages.
